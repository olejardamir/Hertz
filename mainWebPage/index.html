About Hertz Token

Hertz token is a deflationary and stable with a constantly increasing price.

Hertz works by wrapping around Ethereum or any other ERC20 token, and by sharing the earinings equally with all Hertz owners, while acting as a dividend.

The earnings are proportional with the amount of tokens the holders own.

Unless sending a token from one account to another, 2% is the maximum loss a holder will ever have.

Each time a transfer is made from one address to another, 2% fee stays and is shared with all Hertz owners.

Token can be purchased and sold within a contract. Purchasing is free, while selling has a 2% fee unless done on exchanges.

Hertz token has no owner, and is free for anyone to use however they want.

Without the owner, Hertz is decentralized, secure, and free from manipulations.
