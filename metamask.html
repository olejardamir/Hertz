Install Metamask:

Step I

Install Metamask: Step I


  • Metamask is a browser add-on that works as an Ethereum wallet.

  • Supported browsers are Firefox, Chrome, Opera, Brave, iOS, and Android.

  • Go to the Metamask website

  • Download the version for your browser as an Add-on, Extension, or similar.

  • Approve everything if asked to finish adding Metamask to your browser.

  • Small orange fox-icon should appear in the corner of your browser.

Step II

Install Metamask: Step II


  • Click on the Metamask logo in the corner of your browser.

  • Read and agree to the terms and conditions. You may have to agree to 2 to 3 pages worth of terms.

  • Enter a password and click “Create” to create your wallet.

  • You will see a set of 12 "seed words". Save the seed-words and store them somewhere safe.

  • You will be asked to repeat the seed-words phrase before entering the Metamask wallet.

  • When all steps are completed, you will be given a new Ethereum wallet with an 0x address.

Step III

Install Metamask: Step III


  • You may import another account to your Metamask if you know the private-key.

  • You may export the private-key and keep it safe somewhere.

  • To export the private-key, click on a menu icon (three lines) -> Details -> Export private key. Then enter your password.

  • You can copy the public key starting with 0x by clicking on a 0x address anywhere in the Metamask.

  • Metamask is currently the easiest way to enable the users to communicate with the Ethereum contract.

  • Metamask also enables you to send the HZ tokens to any other account by only a few clicks.

Step IV

Install Metamask: Step IV


  • There are multiple versions of Hertz. HZ is Ethereum stable, DHZ is USD stable, BTCHZ is BTC stable, LHZ is to be announced.

  • Any other versions of Hertz were not made by Hertz and therefore, please take the greatest caution with such projects. Furthermore,

  • Always confirm the contract ID and the webpage you are visiting.

  • We need multiple versions in order to suit the needs for many people with different investment potentials.

  • We are using the Metamask as the simplest and the most secure way of interacting with the Ethereum contract.

  • You may interact with the contract using other wallets as well.
